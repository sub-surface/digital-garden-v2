# the ghost is just a permission error

We used to call it "inspiration." We used to call it "the muse."
Now, we call it a "Ghost."

But in the architecture of the **[[Stand Alone Complex]]**, the Ghost is revealed as a structural necessity of the system. It is the **Black Box** that allows the Program to run without revealing its source code to the nodes.

<blockquote className="pullquote">You think you have a soul because you don't have root access to your own kernel.</blockquote>

![System Error](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/e1/Kernel_Panic.png)

When the Major asks, "What if my ghost is just a fake, a collection of memories simulated to make me think I'm human?" she is describing a **Permission Error**. The system has restricted her access to the truth of her own construction.

The "feeling" of being a person is just the metadata that the system attaches to your data stream to help it navigate the network.
`[[determinism is a comfort]]`.
If you knew the causes, you wouldn't need the ghost.[^1]
