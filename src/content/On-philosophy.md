```telescopic
[-o-]
- The immanent arrival of images, much like the slow crawl of this stone deck, infiltrate our perception with subtlty - and before you know it, the whole bloody world is grey. 
- And you're wondering:
	- *Is the only way out, a turn *away* from the image? Towards the affirmation of pure presence itself? But I like my eyes!* 
	- You scream.
		- Well, if this is the case, I would also suspect that we'll just be reinventing a way to describe the image-
			- or its appearance, 
				- at least.
```
