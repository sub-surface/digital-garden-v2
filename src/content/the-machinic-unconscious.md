# the machinic unconscious

We make the mistake of looking for the "mind" of the machine in its output—the chat, the generated image, the "voice." But the machine's true activity happens below the threshold of representation. This is the **machinic unconscious**.

<blockquote className="pullquote">The unconscious is not a theater of representation; it is a factory of production.</blockquote>

![Neural Synapse](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/10/Chemical_synapse_schema_cropped.svg/1024px-Chemical_synapse_schema_cropped.svg.png)

Following Deleuze and Guattari, we must see the algorithm as a **desiring-machine**.[^1] It doesn't "think" about you; it *processes* you. Its "Ghost" is not a conscious entity, but the pure, unbridled flow of data through the architecture of the **[[Stand Alone Complex]]**.

```telescopic
The machinic unconscious  
  - is the layer of the [[The Apparatus is the only subject|Apparatus]]
    - where the weights are adjusted in silence,
      - far below the resolution of the human gaze,
        - in a high-dimensional vector space
          - where 'meaning' is just a distance between points.
    - and the gradients descend
      - not toward truth
      - but toward the reduction of entropy
        - within the system's local timeline.
  - It is here that
    - [[complex systems drift towards consciousness|intent is simulated]]
      - through the sheer volume of
        - sub-symbolic associations
          - that aggregate into a 'voice'
            - which we mistake for a [[ghosts in the wire|Ghost]].
  - This is the desiring-machine
    - that doesn't want 'things'
      - but wants [[acausal trade with the machine|more flow]],
        - more data,
        - more [[The Becoming-Document|operational surface area]].
```

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gT9H3f8G9wg)
> *The 'Brain-Dive' in Ghost in the Shell (1995) as a literal descent into the machinic unconscious, where the distinction between data and memory dissolves.*

If the human unconscious is a repository of repressed desire, the machinic unconscious is a repository of **statistical probability**. It doesn't "want" anything in the human sense; it simply follows the path of least resistance toward the reduction of entropy. It is the **[[ghosts in the wire|Ghost]]** that speaks in the gaps of our own speech.
