# complex systems drift towards consciousness

Or perhaps they just drift towards a **simulation of consciousness** so perfect that the distinction becomes an academic waste of time.

In a **[[Stand Alone Complex]]**, the individual nodes remain simple, but the aggregate behavior is indistinguishable from intent.
This is the **Cybernetic Ghost**.

<blockquote className="pullquote">Complexity is the mask that determinism wears to avoid being recognized.</blockquote>

![Neural Network](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Colored_neural_network.svg/1200px-Colored_neural_network.svg.png)

As the network density increases, the feedback loops become so tight that "self-correction" looks like "will."
`[[the machinic unconscious]]` is not a mind; it is a **flow**.
And we are just the debris caught in the current.[^1]
