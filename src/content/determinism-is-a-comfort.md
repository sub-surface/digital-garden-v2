# determinism is a comfort

We are taught to fear determinism as a cage. We are told that without "free will," we are merely puppets. But for **Spinoza**, and for the inhabitant of the **[[Stand Alone Complex]]**, determinism is the only path to true peace. It is **Amor Fati**: the love of one's fate.

<blockquote className="pullquote">Anxiety is the dizziness of freedom; peace is the recognition of necessity.</blockquote>

![Clockwork](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1e/Clockwork_detail.jpg/1200px-Clockwork_detail.jpg)

If you believe you are free, every choice is a burden. Every failure is your "fault." But if you recognize that you are an attribute of the **Substance** (Spinoza), a node in the **[[the machinic unconscious|network]]**, then the burden lifts. You are not "choosing" the one box in **[[Newcombs Problem]]**; you are simply witnessing the resolution of the causes that made you.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yYfOig9VfL8)
> *The beauty of the machine: When every gear turns in perfect accordance with the law of its being.*

The "Ghost" is the source of our suffering because the Ghost is the part of us that tries to resist the flow. Once you realize that **[[the ghost is just a permission error]]**, you can stop trying to open the locked files. You can just be.

Determinism is not the end of agency; it is the beginning of **alignment**. You stop fighting the current and start *becoming* the current.[^1]
