## How to Use This Site

- **Open internal links as a side panel**: Use `ALT+CLICK` on any link to open it in a stacked card side panel. Each Alt+Click adds a new card. Alt+Click inside a card to insert a new one after it. Press Esc to close the rightmost card.

- **Play some music while you browse**: Use the compact music player at the bottom of your screen. Click the expand arrow to see the full player with cover art, progress bar, and volume control. [Start with this track](music:Eden).

- **Search for topics**: Press `Ctrl+K` to open the search dialog. Or explore connections in the graph view panel on the right.

- **About the site**: This [digital garden](https://github.com/sub-surface/digital-garden) was built with [Quartz](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz/), a static site generator for knowledge gardens and digital gardens.
