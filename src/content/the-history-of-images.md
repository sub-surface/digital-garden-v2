## A Monument to Hi-Fi

I was lucky enough between 2021 and 2023 to work with a talented group of DJs, MCs and generally knees-up ravers in and around Lancaster. Every few weeks I’d get a lift from Rob or Tom, help them haul some speakers into Ulverston and set up for the night to come. Again and again, sweating to death in the Sun’s Basement would bring unforgettable memories and mind-melting hangovers.
