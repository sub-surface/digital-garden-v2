A film that moves at the pace of thought itself. Tarkovsky leads us through the Zone — a place where the laws of physics bend to the weight of desire — and asks whether we truly want what we say we want. The long tracking shots over water and rusted metal feel less like cinema and more like meditation. Every time I return to it, I notice something new in the silence.
