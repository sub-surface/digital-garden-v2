A man walks out of the desert with no memory of who he is, and slowly pieces together the life he left behind. Wenders and Sam Shepard crafted something that feels like the American landscape itself — vast, lonely, saturated in Ry Cooder's slide guitar. The final scene in the peep show booth, where Travis speaks to Jane through one-way glass, is one of the most devastating things ever put on film. A story about how we lose each other and whether we can find our way back.
