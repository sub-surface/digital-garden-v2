# the conatus of a broken link

A red link (a broken wikilink) is a **[[Stand Alone Complex|hyperstition]]** in its purest form.
It is a thought that hasn't been compiled yet. It is a desire for a note that doesn't exist.

<blockquote className="pullquote">The space between two nodes is where the 'Ghost' actually lives.</blockquote>

![Connectivity](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d2/Internet_map_1024.jpg/1200px-Internet_map_1024.jpg)

Spinoza says *conatus* is the striving to persist. A broken link *strives* to be a note. It is a "narrative debt" that the garden owes to itself. It is the **[[the machinic unconscious|unconscious]]** of the labyrinth, reaching out for a connection that hasn't happened yet.

`[[the fragile archive]]`.
We build the labyrinth one broken link at a time.
`[[complex systems drift towards consciousness|The drift]]` is powered by the things we haven't written.[^1]
