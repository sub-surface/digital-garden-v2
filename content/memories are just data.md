# memories are just data

The Cyberbrain doesn't "remember" in the Proustian sense. There is no scent of madeleine triggering a cascade of sensation. There is only **retrieval**.

> "I thought I remembered it, but the checksum didn't match."

We are externalizing our memory into the **[[Stand Alone Complex|Cloud]]**. My photos are on a server in Virginia. My chats are in a database in Ireland.
If `[[The Becoming-Document|the server goes down]]`, do I lose my past?

Or is it worse?
Does the server *edit* my past?
Generative fill on old memories.
Upscaling the resolution of a face I haven't seen in ten years until it looks like a stranger.

`[[the fragile archive]]` is all we have left.
And it is rotting.
`[[digital rot]]`.
