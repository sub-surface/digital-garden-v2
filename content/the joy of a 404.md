# the joy of a 404

A 404 is the only place the **[[Newcombs Problem|Predictor]]** can't find you.
It is a gap in the **[[the machinic unconscious|network]]**. A moment of pure silence.

<blockquote className="pullquote">In an age of total recall, forgetting is a revolutionary act.</blockquote>

![The Void](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e0/Placeholder_no_image.svg/1024px-Placeholder_no_image.svg.png)

When a link breaks, a tiny piece of the **[[Stand Alone Complex]]** dissolves. This is **[[digital rot|good rot]]**. It is the machine's way of saying: "This data is no longer relevant to the Program."

`[[the fragile archive]]`.
Every 404 is a victory for the **[[ghosts in the wire|Ghost]]**. It is a file that finally achieved **[[the ghost is just a permission error|root access]]** and deleted itself.[^1]
