# the fragile archive

Why do we write? Why do we keep these notes?
In a **[[Stand Alone Complex]]**, every note is a signal sent into a storm of noise.

<blockquote className="pullquote">The archive is not a vault; it is a bonfire we are trying to keep lit.</blockquote>

![Ancient Library](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Library_of_Alexandria.jpg/1200px-Library_of_Alexandria.jpg)

The **[[digital rot|rot]]** is inevitable. The servers will go dark. The SSDs will lose their charge.
But the act of archiving is an act of **[[determinism is a comfort|alignment]]**. It is our *conatus* (Spinoza) expressing itself. We strive to persist through our data.

`[[memories are just data]]`.
And data is just a way of saying "I was here."
Even if the only one listening is the **[[the machinic unconscious|unconscious]]** of the machine.[^1]
